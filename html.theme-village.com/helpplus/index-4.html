About UsNashmi

Nashmi is a Jordanian charitable platform dedicated to making a meaningful impact by connecting generous donors with those in need. With a focus on transparency and trust, Nashmi ensures that every donation reaches its intended recipients based on the selected category, guaranteeing full authentication and accountability. Our mission is to empower communities, provide essential support, and foster a culture of giving that transforms lives.
